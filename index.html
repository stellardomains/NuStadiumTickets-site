Seating Guide

Nu Stadium Seating Guide: Best Sections for Every Budget

Nu Stadium's 26,700-seat bowl is designed to put every fan close to the pitch. Here's a breakdown of each seating zone so you can find the perfect spot for your budget and game-day experience.

🏅 Upper Bowl (200 Level)
Sections 201-239 wrap around the entire stadium. Steep rake means clear sightlines even from the top rows. Best value seats in the house — perfect for fans who want atmosphere without the price tag.
From $57
⚽ Lower Bowl (100 Level)
Sections 101-139 put you right on top of the action. West sideline (103-113) and East sideline (127-137) offer the best pitch-level views. Corner sections are great for seeing tactical plays develop.
From $72
🌟 Club Level
Club North (C140-C149) and Club South (C118-C122) offer premium elevated sightlines with exclusive lounge access, in-seat service, and upscale food and drink options. The best of both worlds.
From $185
🔥 Supporters Section (La Familia)
Safe standing sections at the north end — home to The Siege, Vice City 1896, Southern Legion, Nación Rosa y Negro, and IMS.MMXX. The loudest, most electric atmosphere in MLS. Non-stop chanting for 90 minutes.
From $45
💎 Field Suites & Pitchside
ERGO Next Field Level Suites (FS1-FS18) on the north pitchside and East 1-4 on the south side. Literally feet from the touchline. The closest you can get to the action without being on the roster.
From $490
👑 Platinum & Loge Boxes
Platinum Suites (PS26-PS33) at the top of the north stand and Loge Boxes between the supporters and club sections. Private suites with dedicated service — ideal for corporate entertaining or special occasions.
From $350

Transportation

How to Get to Nu Stadium: Parking, Transit & Rideshare

Nu Stadium at Miami Freedom Park is located at 1900 NW 37th Ave in Little Havana, Miami — minutes from Miami International Airport and easily accessible from I-836 and SR-836.

🚗 Driving & Parking
Miami Freedom Park includes dedicated parking within the 131-acre complex. Additional overflow lots nearby. Expect heavy traffic on NW 37th Ave on match days — arrive at least 90 minutes early for the opener.
🚑 Public Transit
The stadium is near several Metrobus routes along NW 37th Ave and Flagler St. The nearest Metrorail station is a short rideshare away. MLS event shuttles are expected from key transit hubs.
📱 Rideshare & Taxi
Dedicated Uber/Lyft drop-off and pick-up zones at Miami Freedom Park. Rideshare is the easiest option — surge pricing likely on match days, so schedule your ride in advance.

🎤 The Atmosphere
Non-stop singing, drums, flags, tifo displays, and coordinated chants. The supporters section is safe standing — no assigned seats, first come first served. Get there early for the best spots near the front.
🎟 What to Know
Supporters sections are safe standing only. Expect to be on your feet the entire match. Bring your voice, respect the culture, and join the chants. Flags and banners are welcome — check stadium policy for size limits.
